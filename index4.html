Best family anime with Awesome Action

It follows the story of Twilight, a renowned spy who is tasked with a mission to infiltrate a prestigious school. To maintain his cover, he adopts Anya, a young girl with psychic abilities, and pretends to be her father. However, unbeknownst to Twilight, Anya is an esper who can read minds. Twilight also needs a wife for his cover, so he meets and marries Yor Briar, an assassin. Together, they form an unconventional family unit, each with their own secrets and hidden agendas, yet bound by genuine love and affection for each other. The series is known for its blend of action, comedy, and heartwarming family dynamics, making it a popular and critically acclaimed work in the manga world.
